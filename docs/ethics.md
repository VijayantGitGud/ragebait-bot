# Ethics and Safety Considerations

This system is designed to provide reflective feedback, not enforce moderation.

## Key Principles

- The system does not accuse users of bad intent.
- Messages are flagged probabilistically and with uncertainty.
- No messages are deleted or hidden.
- Humor is self-referential and non-targeted.

## Intended Use

The bot is intended for opt-in group environments where participants are aware of its presence and purpose.

## Limitations

- Rage-bait detection is inherently subjective.
- Cultural and contextual factors may affect interpretation.
- The system may produce false positives or false negatives.
